About us

Welcome to Pete’s Coffeeshop, a cozy corner in the heart of Portland, Maine, where coffee meets community. At Pete’s, we believe coffee is more than just a drink—it's an experience. Whether you're looking for a quiet spot to savor a cup of our expertly brewed coffee, a comfortable space to co-work and connect with others, or a place to deepen your knowledge about the craft of coffee-making, you'll find it here. Our passionate baristas are not just here to serve you but also to share their love for coffee through interactive courses where you can learn to brew the perfect cup at home. Come in, relax, and let Pete’s Coffeeshop be your go-to spot for great coffee, productivity, and learning in Portland.
